Who We Are

TWAM GLOBAL —
A Name That Travels

Founded in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, TWAM GLOBAL has grown from a regional agricultural trader to a globally recognised export brand. Our name carries with it the promise of purity, precision, and partnership.

We operate at the intersection of India's rich agricultural heritage and the world's demand for quality food commodities. From sourcing to shipping, every step of our process is driven by deep industry expertise and unwavering commitment to excellence.

Our team of agronomists, quality inspectors, and logistics professionals ensures that every order is executed to the highest standard. We don't just export products; we export trust.

Our Process

How We Ensure
Quality at Every Step

1

Careful Farm Selection

We partner only with vetted farmers and agrarian communities across India's prime growing regions — ensuring traceability from field to port.

2

Quality Inspection

Each lot undergoes multi-point quality checks at our in-house lab: moisture, foreign matter, colour, aroma, and chemical analysis against international benchmarks.

3

Processing & Cleaning

State-of-the-art cleaning, sorting, grading, and processing equipment ensures product purity and consistency for every specification.

4

Export-Grade Packaging

Products are packed in export-grade materials tailored to destination regulations — PP bags, HDPE bags, jute bags, and cartons with custom branding.

5

Documentation & Compliance

Our documentation team handles phytosanitary certificates, COAs, country-specific certifications, bill of lading, and all customs paperwork seamlessly.

6

Logistics & Delivery

Partnered with global freight forwarders, we ensure on-time loading, container booking, and real-time shipment tracking until delivery at your port.
